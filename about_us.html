About TMI

Who We Are

We are so blessed to have you visit us at Twainman Ministries Intl. TMI is a prayer and discipleship ministry. We exist to proclaim the Lordship of Jesus to the nations, and fulfill their ordination and ministry. At TMI we have a goal to bring men into their sonship inheritance.We are non- denominational and we embrace everyone regardless of age,race,colour or tribe. We believe in miracles. We are non-denominational and we embrace everyone regardless of age, race or any other characteristic. You do not need to be perfect to fellowship with us at TMI, just be willing to learn and grow. We are committed to ensuring you grow in knowledge by the Word and lead a life of prayer. At TMI we are a strong family system giving all who come a sense of belonging, we love, we care, we support and we build up. We look forward to having you in fellowship with us. Visit our fellowship schedule to learn more about our service times and upcoming events.

OUR MISSION

Proclaiming the Lordship of Jesus Christ and discipling nations.

OUR AFFIRMATION

I am Twainman, I carry the fullness of the Godhead. I am an agent of reconciliation, restoration and redemption in the earth and I reign with Christ.

THE CALL

Romans 10:13-15 For whosoever shall call upon the name of the Lord shall be saved. How then shall they call on him in whom they have not believed? and how shall they believe in him of whom they have not heard? and how shall they hear without a preacher? And how shall they preach, except they be sent? as it is written, How beautiful are the feet of them that preach the gospel of peace, and bring glad tidings of good things!

OUR MANDATE

To reconcile and restore every creature to God according to His perfect will, predestination and good pleasure in Christ Jesus unto a perfect man Ephesians 1:9-10; 2:15. At TMI we have a mandate to serve this generation and generations to come with the power, glory and the wisdom of God with signs, wonders and miracles.

THE ASSIGNMENT

Acts 26:18, To open their eyes, and to turn them from darkness to light, and from the power of Satan unto God, that they may receive forgiveness of sins, and inheritance among them which are sanctified by faith that is in Christ. We have a lifetime assignment to turn sinners and whole cities to Christ. We are committed to bringing believers into spiritual maturity, sonship inheiritance and conformity to the image of Jesus Christ.

A PEOPLE FOR GOD

We are a non denominational prayer and discipleship ministry. We are a prophetic army with an Apostolic mandate to proclaim and manifest the Lordship of Jesus as the Christ with full expressions of the kingdom of God amongst us and to make disciples of nations in every place. Our passion for God is deep Our vision is clear Our goal is Christ;to be like him in all things Our experience with God is real and personal. We grow in Christ in all things We live as a family We work as a team We are the circumcision who worship God in the spirit and have no confidence in the flesh We believe in the manifestations of the sons of God to salvage the creation from the bondage of corruption. preparing the bride-The Church for Christ and enthroning Jesus Christ passion to see whole cities and nations turn to Christ.

Meet The Lead Pastors

PST. PATRICK AND PST. MRS. HAPPY EVBOMOEN

Pastor Patrick Evbomoen is the lead Pastor and visionary of Twainman Ministry International. He is called and sent of God with heaven’s mandate to preach reconciliation, restoration and redemption. Pastor Patrick has been in ministry for well over 20years, an apt teacher of the word of God with a great passion for prayers, discipleship and to see men come into the fullness of the stature of Christ. He is a pioneer of several interdenominational impactful meetings. Many who have stayed under his ministrations received healings and astounding miracles by the mighty hand of God. He is a writer, author and has a great love for music. He has a penchant for excellence, diligence and humility which has endeared him to certain patriarchs of the gospel and seasoned men of God. He has and is still enjoying tremendous grace from these relationships. Pastor Patrick is prosperously and joyfully married to Mrs Happy Evbomoen who co-pastors the ministry with him. They are blessed with four biological children Great, Joel, Jason and Bethel serving alongside with them in the ministry.
